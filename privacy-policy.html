Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

At Brillix Technologies, we are committed to safeguarding your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and store your data. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the information we gather when you visit our website or engage with our services. By using our website, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services. The data we gather falls into the following categories:

  • Personal Information: When you fill out our contact form or reach out to us directly, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, and any details you include in your message. This information is provided voluntarily and is used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect certain information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent on each page, click patterns, and referral sources. This data is gathered through cookies and third-party analytics tools to help us understand user behaviour and improve the site experience.
  • Technical Data: Our servers automatically log technical information such as your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, screen resolution, and language preferences. This information helps us ensure compatibility and optimise performance across different devices and platforms.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for specific, legitimate purposes. We do not use your data beyond what is necessary to operate and improve our services effectively.

  • Responding to Inquiries: When you submit a contact form or send us an email, we use your personal information to respond to your questions, provide quotes, and discuss potential projects.
  • Improving Our Services: Usage and technical data allow us to identify areas of our website that need improvement, understand which services are of greatest interest, and optimise the overall user experience.
  • Analytics and Reporting: We use aggregated, anonymised data to generate internal reports on website traffic, user demographics, and engagement metrics. These insights guide our business decisions and content strategy.
  • Marketing Communications: With your explicit consent, we may send you occasional updates about our services, blog posts, or company news. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us or using the unsubscribe link provided in our emails.

Data Sharing

Brillix Technologies does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We believe your data belongs to you, and we treat it with the utmost respect and care.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party service providers who assist us with hosting, email delivery, and analytics. These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, in response to a valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Brillix Technologies, our users, or the public.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and gather analytical data. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognise returning visitors and understand usage patterns.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the basic functioning of the website, including session management and security features.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used by Google Analytics to collect anonymised data about how visitors use our site.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings to provide a more personalised experience.

For more detailed information about the cookies we use and how to manage them, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website is served over SSL/TLS encryption, ensuring that all data transmitted between your browser and our servers is securely encrypted.

We maintain strict access controls so that only authorised personnel can access personal data, and only when necessary for legitimate business purposes. While no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, we continuously review and update our security practices to align with current best standards.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data. We are committed to honouring these rights promptly and transparently:

  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you at any time.
  • Right to Correction: If any of your personal information is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request that we correct or update it.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal data from our systems, subject to any legal obligations that may require us to retain certain information.
  • Right to Opt Out: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us at any time without affecting your ability to use our services.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at jerinpjpositive@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

Our website integrates with several third-party services to provide enhanced functionality and analytics. Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects anonymised data including page views, session duration, and geographic location. This data is processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy.
  • EmailJS: Our contact form uses EmailJS to deliver your messages to our team. EmailJS processes the information you submit in the form, including your name, email address, and message content.
  • CDN Providers: We use content delivery networks to serve fonts, scripts, and other static assets. These providers may collect technical data such as your IP address as part of delivering content to your browser.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and post a notice on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Continued use of our website after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms. If you disagree with any modifications, please discontinue use of our website and contact us to request the deletion of your data.
